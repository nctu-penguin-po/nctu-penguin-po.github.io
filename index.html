Who we are

NCTU AUV Team was started in July 2018, with the goal to develop an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle(AUV) to participate in competitions i.e. SAUVC (Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Challenge) and RoboSub. Led by Dr. Lua Kim Boon, Dr. Teng-Hu Cheng, and Dr. Chia-Hung Dylan Tsai, we has participated in the 2019 March SAUVC in Singapore Polytechnic, and is currently working on making our vehicle, Po’s performance better.

The aim of the team is to participate and win in The Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Challenge & RoboSub. Our mission is to combine the knowledge of Fluid Mechanics, Automatic Control and Mechanical Designs in the process of developing an underwater vehicle that can complete the competition tasks autonomously.

Mechanical Design

By using CAD software, we are able to draft all our technical and engineering drawings with the aid of computer instead of hand drawings. We made all our team's ideas coming true and making modification, analysis or optimization of our AUV design.

Hardware

Prepare to get hands dirty! All motors and electronic components you see are our business. Our job will need to understand how each electrical components work and how to maintain and substitute them. We will get bunch of things to solder, so we won’t ever get bored and our soldering skill will be EXTREMELY skillful. Endless hands-on and acknowledge are waiting for you. Keep in mind, “Failure is not an option! ”.

Software

Based on the Robot Operating System (ROS), we are able to communicate with all sensors and achieve various of functions (e.g. visual identification, PID control, hydrophone detection, path decision and etc.) for our AUV, making it become smarter and functional.
